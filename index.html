Border Surveillance Map

A collaborative map to crowdsource data on new border technologies appearing in our communities.

This platform collects user submitted sightings of border related technologies. By crowdsourcing data collection, we can document a wider area and build a more complete picture of surveillance deployments. The map displays two main types of information: markers indicating different device or camera types, and an additional layer showing their estimated coverage ranges. Use the map to explore known deployments, submit new sightings, and help maintain an up to date shared dataset.
